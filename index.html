The World's Largest Rodent

I first laid my eyes upon this great creature when I first visited the San Diego Zoo years ago. Ever since, I have claimed the capybara to be my ultimate spirit animal. Here are some fun facts:

1. Capybaras are the largest rodents on Earth! They are about 3.2-4.2 feet in length and can weigh up to 174 pounds!

2. They usually consume about 6 to 8 pounds of grasses and aquatic plants a day.

3. Capybaras have been used as guide animals.
